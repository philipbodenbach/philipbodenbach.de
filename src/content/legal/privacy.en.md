The website contains e-mail links.

If you contact me by e-mail, I process the information you provide, in particular your e-mail address, the content of the message and the time of communication. The processing is carried out to handle your request.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR if your request relates to a contract or pre-contractual measures. In all other cases, the legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR; the legitimate interest lies in responding to your request.

The website offers a switch for light and dark design. If you change the mode, your selection is stored in your browser's local storage under the key `pb-theme`. This storage is used solely to apply your selected display setting again on your next visit. The legal basis for any processing of personal data is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

This website is provided over encrypted HTTPS. This protects data transmitted between your browser and the website according to the state of the art.

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary for the respective purpose. It is then deleted unless statutory retention obligations, security interests or legal claims prevent deletion.

Business correspondence may be retained in accordance with commercial and tax law requirements.

To exercise your rights, a message to info@philipbodenbach.de is sufficient.
